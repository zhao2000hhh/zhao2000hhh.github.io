Research on automated presentation (PPT) generation is fragmented across input settings with inconsistent evaluation. UniPPTBench proposes a unified benchmark covering four representative scenarios — vague instructions, long documents, multimodal documents, and multi-source inputs. We design a capability-aware evaluation protocol, **UniPPTEval**, that supports both cross-scenario comparison and scenario-specific grounding evaluation, and provide transparent reference baselines for reproducible comparison.

Under review at **ACM MM 2026**. First author.
