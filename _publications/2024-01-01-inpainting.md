Real-world image inpainting often suffers from poor generalization when input resolution and aspect ratio vary. To address this, we propose an inpainting method built on implicit neural representations, improving the model's adaptability to different input sizes and high-resolution scenes.

Published in *Pattern Recognition* (CAS Tier-1 journal). First author.
