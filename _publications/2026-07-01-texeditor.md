Text-driven texture editing struggles to balance appearance change with geometric structure preservation. TexEditor improves structural consistency from both data and training perspectives: we build a high-quality synthetic dataset, **TexBlender**, to provide structural priors, and design a reinforcement learning method, **StructureNFT**, that transfers these priors to real-world scenes. We further construct **TexBench**, a real-world evaluation benchmark for texture editing. On this sub-task, TexEditor surpasses the commercial model Nano Banana Pro.

Accepted to **ICML 2026**. First author.
